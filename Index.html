About So ShinaiLit

Where Nail Art Meets Convenience

Founded in 2023, So ShinaiLit began with a simple idea: everyone deserves to have beautiful, creative nails without spending hours at a salon or breaking the bank.

Our press-on nails are crafted with high-quality materials and attention to detail. Each set is designed to look like a professional salon manicure but can be applied in minutes at home.

We're passionate about self-expression through nail art and believe that your nails should be as unique as you are.
